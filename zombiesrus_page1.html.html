Welcome to Zombies 'R Us. We are your one stop shop in beginning your Zombie Acopalypse Preparedness Plan (ZAPP). While there are still many disbelievers out there, let's take a few moments to check out the facts.


Last year, the CDC released a Preparedness 101:Zombie Apocalypse. This was the CDC's guide in helping the citizens create an emergency plan. Think this was one big joke?


Just two years before, a San Antonian woman killed and ate the brains of her three week old baby. If that wasn't bad enough, she decided to eat a few of his toes as well.


In May of this year, two seperate Zombie attacks took place. The first included the face of the victim being completely eaten. The second was a homeless man trying to literally eat a police officer's hand.


Jump forward to June 2012. Eight countes in Maine came together to do a Zombie Apocalypse Medical Response training day. Different stages of the infection were even categorized for the Medical Response teams to know how to deal with each situation.


And just when we thought this Zombie craze was finished, last week a Pennsylvania man jumped out a second-story window and attacked a woman, gnawing at her head. Luckily, the woman had been walking with another female friend, and both women were able to get away. The police were notified.


Whether you like it or not, are you prepared for the Zombie Apocalypse?
